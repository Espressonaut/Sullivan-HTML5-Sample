Management Companies

The data collection software uses management companies to assign contacts to properties, and to configure parsing rules for file imports. Once a management company has been created, it may be accessed as required.

Creating and Accessing Management Companies

Create a New Management Company

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Click +Add Management Company
  3. Enter name of new management company
  4. Click Add Company

Access Existing Management Company

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Select management company in drop-down list

Assigning and Reassigning Management Companies

After a property has been entered into the data collection software, it must be assigned to a management company. Properties may also be reassigned to other management companies as needed.

Assign or Reassign Management Companies

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Assign Companies
  2. Select current management company from Management Company drop-down list
    • "Unassigned" displays all properties not currently assigned to any management company
  3. Check boxes for properties to be assigned
  4. Select new management company from Reassignment Company drop-down list
  5. Enter reassignment date
    • if set to future, entry displays in gray and remains in place until appropriate date
  6. Click Reassign
