Assign Companies

Once a property has been entered into the data collection software, it must be assigned to a management company. Properties may also be reassigned to other management companies as needed.

Assign or Reassign Management Companies

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Assign Companies
  2. Select current management company from Management Company drop-down list
    • "Unassigned" displays all properties not currently assigned to any management company
  3. Check boxes for properties to be assigned
  4. Select new management company from Reassignment Company drop-down list
  5. Enter reassignment date
    • if set to future, entry displays in gray and remains in place until appropriate date
  6. Click Reassign
