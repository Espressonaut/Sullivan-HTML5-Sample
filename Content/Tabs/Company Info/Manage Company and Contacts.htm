Manage Company and Contacts

This screen lets the user create new management companies or access existing management companies. It also allows contacts to be created within, or deleted from, a management company.

Create a New Management Company

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Click +Add Management Company
  3. Enter name of new management company
  4. Click Add Company

Access Existing Management Company

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Select management company in drop-down list

Create a Contact

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Select management company
  3. Click +Add Contact to create new rows for contact entries
  4. Enter First Name, Last Name, Phone, and Email for contact(s)
  5. Click Save

Delete an Unassigned Contact

  1. Navigate to Company Info > Manage Company and Contacts
  2. Click Delete next to contact to be removed
    • To undo deletion, click before saving
  3. Click Save
