Reprocessing Trial Balances

It is sometimes necessary to update previously loaded trial balances with new mapping or data changes. Once the change has been made in the system, it must be reprocessed.

Reprocess Trial Balances

  1. Navigate to Sheet Management > Reprocess
  2. Select Trial Balance in drop-down list on right hand side of the screen
  3. Select management company in drop-down list
  4. Select property in drop-down list
    • "All Properties" will reprocess data for each property for the specified management company
  5. Select month and year
    • All data for selected properties will be reprocessed and validated from selected date to current period
  6. Click Reprocess
  7. Navigate to Home > Tracking and verify successful import
